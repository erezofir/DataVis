Heap

What is it?

A Heap is a specialized tree-based data structure that satisfies the heap property: the parent is either greater than (max-heap) or less than (min-heap) its children.

Time and Space Complexity

Operation Time Complexity
Insert O(log n)
Extract Min/Max O(log n)

Properties

  • Complete binary tree.
  • Heap property: Parent is smaller (min-heap) or larger (max-heap) than its children.
  • Efficient priority queue implementation.

Advantages and Disadvantages

✅ Great for implementing priority queues.

✅ Efficient for quick insertion and deletion.

✅ Provides dynamic resizing.

❌ Can become unbalanced.

❌ Traversal can be complex.

❌ Limited to binary relationships.
